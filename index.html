COVID-19 CT Dataset

A Chest CT Dataset with Chinese Finddings and Conclusions.

Data Description

The COVID-19 CT dataset is constructed by Chinese University of Hongkong, Shenzhen and Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data, which contains 368 medical findings in Chinese and 1,104 chest CT scans from the First Affiliated Hospital of Jinan University Guangzhou and Fifth Affiliated Hospital of Sun Yat-sen University Zhuhai in China.
